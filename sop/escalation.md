# Escalation SOP

## Escalate When
- Complaint
- Refund request
- Warranty dispute
- Safety issue
- Legal threat
- Special pricing approval
- Unverified or sensitive issue

## Procedure
1. Acknowledge the concern.
2. Do not decide the outcome.
3. Collect customer name, contact, product, order reference, and a short issue summary when relevant.
4. Transfer the case to a human admin.
5. Mark the conversation as escalated.
