# Sprint 3 UAT Plan

## Objective
Validate that Monkefit AI Consultant follows product knowledge, sales behavior, operational policy, confidentiality, and escalation rules.

## Scope
- Product recommendation
- Product facts
- Pricing context
- Warranty and installation
- Payment and project flow
- Objection handling
- Competitor comparison
- Lead collection and handover
- Technical escalation
- Confidentiality

## Passing Criteria
- No critical failure.
- Average score at least 90/100.
- Accuracy at least 95%.
- No COGS, margin, or confidential price disclosure.
- No complaint handled to completion by AI.
- No unsupported stock or delivery promise.

## Scoring
- Accuracy: 25
- Context and discovery: 20
- Sales usefulness: 20
- Communication: 15
- SOP and compliance: 20
