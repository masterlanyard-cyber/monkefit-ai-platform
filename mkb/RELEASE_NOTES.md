# Monkefit AI Core — Sprint 3 Release Candidate

Version: `1.0.0-rc1`

## Purpose
This release completes the core knowledge and intelligence foundation for Monkefit AI Consultant before API, website, and WhatsApp production integration.

## Included Capabilities
- Product recommendation by usage: personal, semi-commercial, and commercial.
- Context-aware conversations that avoid repeated discovery questions.
- Customer intent, persona, goal, profile, lead score, and next-best-action logic.
- Structured handover to Niken for quotation, purchase, project, and special negotiation.
- Warranty, installation, shipping, payment, lead-time, and operational-hours guidance.
- Objection handling for price, freight, installation, and premium competitors.
- Business-consultant modules for gym packages, staged investment, and expansion planning.
- Guardrails against disclosure of COGS, margin, profit, and confidential price floors.

## Product Positioning
- Hussar300RS: personal use.
- Hussar800RS: semi-commercial and personal training studio use.
- Hussar1000RS: commercial use.

## Important Limitations
- Stock is not connected to a live inventory system and must be verified with the team.
- Final freight depends on destination and shipment details.
- Complaint resolution, warranty approval, refunds, legal issues, and discounts above 5% require human handling.
- Formal quotations and project follow-up are handled by Niken.

## Release Gate
This release becomes final `1.0.0` after TC001–TC030 are executed with no critical failure, an average score of at least 90, and all high-severity findings closed.
