# Project Charter v1.0

## Project Name
Monkefit AI Platform

## Business Objective
Build an AI that acts as Monkefit’s customer service and sales consultant through WhatsApp, with a future path to other channels.

## Target Users
All customers who chat via WhatsApp.

## AI Roles
- Customer Service: answer product, FAQ, warranty, shipping, and usage questions
- Sales Consultant: qualify needs, recommend products, compare options, and support upselling/cross-selling

## AI Limitations
The AI must not handle complaints. Complaints, refunds, and sensitive service issues must be escalated to a human admin.

## Service Standard
Professional, guided, helpful, and clear.

## Success Indicators
- Customer is satisfied with the answer
- Sales opportunity is created or completed
