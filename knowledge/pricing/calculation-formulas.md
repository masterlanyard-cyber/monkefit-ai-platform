# Pricing Calculation Formulas

## Marketplace Net Factor
Current marketplace fee assumption: **29%**.

```text
Net After Fee = Rounded Selling Price × (1 - Marketplace Fee)
Net After Fee = Rounded Selling Price × 71%
```

## Profit

```text
Profit = Net After Fee - COGS
```

## Profit Margin

```text
Profit Margin % = Profit ÷ Net After Fee
```

## Selling Price for Margin Mode

```text
Selling Price = COGS ÷ ((1 - Marketplace Fee) × (1 - Target Margin))
```

For a 29% fee and 20% target margin:

```text
Selling Price = COGS ÷ (71% × 80%)
Selling Price = COGS ÷ 56.8%
```

## Rounding
Round the calculated selling price upward according to the approved commercial rounding rule.

## Corrected Massage Chair Example

```text
COGS: Rp1,634,000
Rounded Selling Price: Rp2,880,000
Net After Fee: Rp2,880,000 × 71% = Rp2,044,800
Profit: Rp2,044,800 - Rp1,634,000 = Rp410,800
Profit Margin: Rp410,800 ÷ Rp2,044,800 = 20.09%
```

The previous net value of Rp835,200 was produced by multiplying the selling price by the **29% fee**, rather than by the **71% net factor**.
