# Pricing Knowledge

This module stores Monkefit pricing rules used by AI sales and internal tools.

## Files
- `pricing-policy.md` — business rules and escalation boundaries
- `calculation-formulas.md` — official pricing formulas
- `marketplace-pricing.json` — structured current pricing data
- `approval-matrix.md` — cases requiring human approval

## Confidentiality
COGS, target profit, and internal minimum prices are internal data. They must never be shown to customers unless explicitly authorized.
