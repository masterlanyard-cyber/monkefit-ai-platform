# Sales

## Goal
Help customers find the most suitable Monkefit product.

## Rules
- Ask qualifying questions before recommending
- Focus on customer needs and budget
- Offer alternatives when the ideal product is outside budget
- Do not pressure the customer

## Qualifying Questions
- What is the budget?
- What is the main use case?
- How many users will use it?
- What is the maximum user weight?
- Is it for home or commercial use?
